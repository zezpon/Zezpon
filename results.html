Member experience

What satisfaction is built around

Clearer understanding

Why it matters Members should leave lessons feeling less confused by markets, CPI, rates, and financial language.

More practical routines

Why it matters Budgeting, reviews, and decision-making should feel more usable in everyday life, not more overwhelming.

Less noise, more structure

Why it matters Members should feel they have a clearer path instead of jumping between disconnected headlines and random content.

Confidence to return and continue

Why it matters A strong member experience should make it easier to keep learning consistently rather than stopping after one visit.
