Privacy

Privacy policy

This policy explains how Zezpon handles personal information in connection with member accounts, contact requests, protected content access, and account support.

What information Zezpon may collect

  • Contact details such as your name and email address
  • Account details needed to create and manage your membership
  • Login, verification, password reset, and session activity linked to your account
  • Profile details you choose to add or update in your member settings
  • Content and support enquiries you send through Zezpon contact channels

How information is used

  • To create and manage your account and membership access
  • To verify your email address and support password recovery
  • To deliver member content according to your plan and account permissions
  • To respond to support, partnership, and account enquiries
  • To maintain site security, review suspicious activity, and support platform administration

Access control and security

  • Account access is controlled through authenticated sessions and plan-based permissions
  • Password handling takes place on the server rather than in your browser storage
  • Protected member media and content are restricted according to account access rules
  • Administrative actions may be logged for security and service management purposes

Email and account communications

  • Zezpon may send service emails such as verification messages, password reset links, account notices, and important membership updates
  • Transactional account and security emails are part of providing the service and are distinct from optional marketing communications
  • If marketing emails are introduced later, Zezpon should provide clear opt-in and unsubscribe controls

Storage and service providers

  • Zezpon may use third-party providers for hosting, storage, email delivery, analytics, and related technical infrastructure
  • Protected media may be stored on local server infrastructure or S3-compatible object storage depending on the live deployment
  • Member data should be limited to what is reasonably required to operate the service, secure accounts, and support members

Your choices

  • You may request updates to your account details through your member profile or support contact channels
  • You may request assistance if you believe your account data is inaccurate or your access permissions are incorrect
  • You may choose whether to continue using the service if you do not agree with future policy updates
