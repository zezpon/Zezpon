Security

How Zezpon protects members

Zezpon uses layered controls to protect member accounts, restrict paid content by plan, and support safer login, recovery, and admin access.

What is protected

  • Member accounts and password-based sign-in
  • Paid content access for Basic and Premium members
  • Admin-only tools for member and content management
  • Email verification and password reset workflows

Current protections

  • Passwords are handled server-side instead of being stored in the browser
  • Member access is enforced through authenticated sessions and plan checks
  • Session cookies are issued with HttpOnly and SameSite protections
  • Security headers help reduce framing, sniffing, and transport risks
  • Rate limiting helps slow repeated auth and admin requests
  • Admin actions and content changes are written to audit logs

Account recovery and verification

  • Email verification links are generated per account and expire after a limited time
  • Password reset links are time-limited and intended for one recovery flow at a time
  • Email delivery supports verification and recovery across the account flow

Good security habits

  • Use HTTPS across the full site
  • Keep storage, email, and admin access tightly controlled
  • Review account activity and content permissions regularly
  • Rotate privileged credentials when needed
  • Scale storage and database systems as membership grows

Member checklist

  • Use a strong unique password
  • Verify your email after signup
  • Reset your password if you suspect account access issues
  • Contact support if content access looks incorrect
